About SVITS

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1998

Our History & Journey

Founded in 1998, Shri Vaishnav Institute of Technology & Science was established with a vision to create a center for academic excellence in engineering and technology in Central India. From our humble beginnings with just two departments, we have grown into a leading institution with multiple disciplines, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a vibrant community of over 4,000 students.

To empower students with cutting-edge knowledge, technical skills, and strong ethical values, preparing them to become innovative problem-solvers...

Our Vision

To be a nationally recognized leader in technical education and research, fostering an environment of creativity and intellectual growth to shape a better future...
