Who We Are

Shri Vaishnav Institute of Technology & Science

Established in 1995, SVITS has emerged as a pioneer in technical education in Madhya Pradesh.

Managed by the Shri Vaishnav Shekshanik Evam Parmarthik Trust, our institute is dedicated to providing world-class education. We are approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal.

With a sprawling green campus of 25 acres, modern laboratories, and a dedicated placement cell, we ensure holistic development for every student.
