About Dr. Sharma

Dr. Aman Sharma is a distinguished Professor and the Head of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at SVITS. With over 20 years of experience in academia and research, his expertise lies in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing.

He holds a Ph.D. in AI from IIT Bombay and has published over 50 research papers in international journals and conferences. Dr. Sharma is dedicated to mentoring students and fostering a research-oriented environment within the department.

Areas of Expertise

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Data Science
