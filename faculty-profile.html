About Dr. Aman Sharma

Dr. Aman Sharma is a distinguished academician and researcher with over 15 years of experience in the field of Computer Science. He completed his Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from IIT Bombay. His primary research interests include Machine Learning, Data Analytics, and Cloud Computing.

He has guided more than 10 Ph.D. scholars and has published numerous research papers in reputed international journals. At SVITS, he leads the "AI Innovation Cell" and mentors students for national-level hackathons.
