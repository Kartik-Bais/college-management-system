Annual Tech Fest "Innovate 2025"

Event Details

Date: 15th December 2025
Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Location: SVITS Auditorium & Main Ground

"Innovate 2025" is the flagship technical festival of Shri Vaishnav Institute of Technology & Science. It's a platform for students to showcase their technical prowess, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Highlights

  • Robo-Wars: The ultimate robotic combat.
  • Code-a-Thon: 24-hour coding challenge.
  • Project Expo: Showcase of innovative student projects.
  • Guest lectures from industry experts.
