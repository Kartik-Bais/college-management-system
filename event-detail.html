Innovate 2026: The Future is Here

Annual Technical Festival of SVITS

About the Event

"Innovate 2026" is the flagship technical festival of Shri Vaishnav Institute of Technology & Science. It is a platform for students from across the country to showcase their technical prowess, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

This year, we are focusing on the theme "Sustainable Technology for a Better Tomorrow". The event will witness participation from over 50 colleges and will feature a plethora of competitions, workshops, and expert talks.
