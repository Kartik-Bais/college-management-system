Program Overview

The B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering at SVITS is designed to mold students into world-class software professionals. Our curriculum is updated regularly to include cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Internet of Things (IoT).

We focus on a balanced approach between theoretical concepts and practical application through our modern labs and project-based learning methodology.
