About the Program

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at SVITS is a hub of innovation. Our program is designed to provide students with a robust foundation in computing, software development, and advanced topics like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity.

We focus on a hands-on, project-based learning approach. Our state-of-the-art labs for AI, data science, and network security provide an unparalleled environment for students to experiment and build cutting-edge solutions.

Key Subjects & Syllabus

  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems (DBMS)
  • Operating Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity & Network Protocols
  • Cloud Computing & DevOps
