About the Library

The SVITS Central Library is a state-of-the-art facility spread over 1500 sq. meters. It is fully automated with KOHA Integrated Library Management Software. We provide access to a vast collection of text books, reference books, periodicals, and digital resources including IEEE, ASME, and DELNET.
