B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Admissions to B.Tech are conducted through MP DTE Counselling based on JEE Main Score or 12th Merit.

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Maths.
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).
  • Valid JEE Main Scorecard (Preferred).
Note: Lateral Entry is available for Diploma holders directly in 2nd Year.

M.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Specializations available in Computer Science, Digital Communications, and VLSI Design.

  • B.E. / B.Tech in relevant branch.
  • Valid GATE Score is mandatory for scholarship.
  • Sponsored candidates need 2 years experience.

MBA Eligibility Criteria

Dual specialization offered in Marketing, Finance, HR, and Systems.

  • Graduation in any discipline with min 50%.
  • Valid CMAT Score (Counseling via DTE).
